About Me

Hi, I'm Patrick Alunya, a passionate Full Stack Developer with a keen eye for detail and a drive for continuous learning. I specialize in front-end and back-end development, focusing on creating intuitive and visually appealing websites and applications.

With a background skills and experience in Frontend Designs and Backend development, I have delved much into API endpoints developments and integrating frontend and backend using API endpoints. My expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript,PHP, MySQL and frameworks express.js and Node.js. I'm enthusiastic about UI/UX design and strive to create user experiences that are both functional and delightful.

Outside of coding, I participate in hackathons, continuously pushing my skills and collaborating with other tech enthusiasts. I believe in the power of technology to make a positive impact and am committed to contributing to meaningful projects and communities.

If you're looking for a developer to bring your ideas to life or want to chat about technology and design, feel free to reach out. Let's build something amazing together!
